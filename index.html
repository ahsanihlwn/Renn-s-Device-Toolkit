Renn's Device Toolkit
v1.0.3

R.D.T is a toolkit for power users and developers. It lets you flash single or multiple partitions, flash recovery/vendor_boot, check & switch A/B slots, and manage devices over ADB & Fastboot. All in one place!.

Fast & Lightweight

ADB/Fastboot operations without hassle, responsive UI, minimal distractions.

Safe & Controlled

Clear status, progress indicators, and protection to avoid wrong flashing.

Simple & Developer Modes

Simple mode for casual users, developer mode for advanced modders.

Multi Flash

Flash multiple partitions in sequence with the order you set.

Recovery Tools

Flash vendor_boot/recovery, reboot, and sideload ZIPs with clear indicators.

Easy to Use

Clean and straightforward interface, designed to be simple and easy to use while tinkering.
